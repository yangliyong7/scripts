# Friends S05E23-24 (Vegas Finale) 口语表达笔记

## 1. 情绪与反应
- **Spoil**: 破坏、糟蹋（好事）。
  - *Example*: I didn't want anything to **spoil** our anniversary.
- **Cross a line**: 越界、做得太过分。
  - *Example*: You drew on me while I was sleeping? You have **crossed a line**.
- **On fire**: 状态极佳、运气爆棚（通常指赌博或运动）。
  - *Example*: Keep rolling! You are **on fire**!

## 2. 决策与行动
- **Do me a favor**: 帮我个忙。
  - *Example*: Can you **do me a favor** and check my PIN number?
- **Lay down ground rules**: 制定基本规则。
  - *Example*: Before we start, I want to **lay down a couple of ground rules**.
- **Go through with**: 完成、坚持做完（尤指困难或令人不愉快的事）。
  - *Example*: I won't **go through with** this if it raises the question of "us."
- **Shut down**: 关闭、停摆（指项目或生意）。
  - *Example*: The movie **shut down** because they ran out of money.
- **Swoop in**: 突然冲入、迅速介入。
  - *Example*: A lurker waits for you to leave and then **swoops in** to steal your jackpot.

## 3. 社交与关系
- **Run into sb.**: 偶遇某人。
  - *Example*: I **ran into** Richard yesterday at the store.
- **Ditch sb.**: 抛弃、甩掉某人（非正式）。
  - *Example*: You are not gonna **ditch** me again like you did in London.
- **Make up**: 和好、言归于好。
  - *Example*: Hey, you guys **made up**? That's great.
- **Get a room**: 开个房间去（调侃情侣在公共场合过于亲密）。
  - *Example*: You two are kissing so much. Why don't you **get a room**?

## 4. 描述事物
- **Stunt double**: 特技替身演员。
  - *Example*: That wasn't me, that was my **stunt double**.
- **Identical twin**: 同卵双胞胎（剧中 Joey 戏称为 hand twin）。
  - *Example*: I found my **identical** hand **twin** at the casino.
- **Pricey**: 昂贵的、花钱多的。
  - *Example*: Macadamia nuts are some **pricey** nuts.
- **Overcharge**: 收费过高、敲竹杠。
  - *Example*: They really **overcharge** you for drinks in the hotel.

## 5. 实用短句
- **What the hell took you so long?**: 你到底怎么花了这么久？（带点抱怨）
  - *Example*: **What the hell took you so long?** We've been waiting for an hour.
- **Check me out**: 看我/瞧瞧我（引起别人注意）。
  - *Example*: **Check me out**, I'm walking around naked!
- **It's all on me**: 我请客/费用全算我的。
  - *Example*: Don't worry about the bill, **it's all on me**.
- **Worth a shot**: 值得一试。
  - *Example*: Asking him for money was a long shot, but it was **worth a shot**.
- **Take it as a sign**: 把它当作一个征兆/暗示。
  - *Example*: If we roll an eight, let's **take it as a sign** and get married.

## 6. 剧情词汇
- **Gladiator**: 角斗士
- **Pin number**: ATM 密码
- **Anniversary**: 周年纪念
- **Jackpot**: 头奖、累积奖金
- **Wedding chapel**: 婚礼小教堂（拉斯维加斯常见的快速结婚场所）
