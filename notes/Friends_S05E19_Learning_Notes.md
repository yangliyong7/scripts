# Friends S05E19 口语表达笔记

## 1. 情绪与反应
- **Get nervous**: 变得紧张。
  - *Example*: Ross just **gets really nervous when he's flirting**.
- **Freak out**: 抓狂、惊慌失措。
  - *Example*: Aren't we gonna **freak her out** soon if we keep talking this way?
- **Feel terrible**: 感觉很糟糕、内疚。
  - *Example*: I **feel so terrible** about losing your earring.
- **Be proud of**: 为...感到骄傲。
  - *Example*: I am so **proud of Joey**, he's going to be on Law & Order!

## 2. 决策与行动
- **Pull some strings**: 动用关系、走后门。
  - *Example*: I **made a few calls, pulled some strings**, and they agreed to seat us.
- **Turn on the charm**: 施展魅力。
  - *Example*: When she comes back, I'll **turn on the Charm-O-Ross**.
- **Swoop in**: 突然介入、抢走。
  - *Example*: This is a girl that I really like and you had to **swoop in there**!
- **Cut back**: 减少、削减（已在E18中出现）。
- **Step aside**: 退让（已在E17/E18中出现）。

## 3. 社交与关系
- **Flirt with sb.**: 与某人调情、勾搭。
  - *Example*: Chandler was totally **flirting with the hot delivery girl**!
- **Hit on sb.**: 追求某人、向某人示好。
  - *Example*: If anyone should be **hitting on her**, it's the guy who's single.
- **See other people**: 与其他人交往（分手后）。
  - *Example*: We broke up two years ago; I think it's okay that we **see other people**.
- **Borrow stuff**: 借东西。
  - *Example*: I'm not allowed to **borrow her stuff** because I lose it!

## 4. 描述事物
- **Hot girl/boy**: 火辣的/帅气的男孩/女孩。
  - *Example*: This **hot girl** in Ross's building is **flirting with me**.
- **Casual conversation**: 随意的对话。
  - *Example*: That was just **casual conversation** between two people, not flirting.
- **Big scene**: 重要场景（演戏）。
  - *Example*: My **big scene** is coming up. This is my moment!
- **Body bag**: 尸袋（剧中Joey担心自己的角色会死）。
  - *Example*: You see that **body bag** right there? I'm in it.
- **Pathetic**: 可怜的、悲哀的。
  - *Example*: That's **pathetic**! Guys think every woman who flirts wants to sleep with them.

## 5. 实用短句
- **What the hell was that?**: 这到底是怎么回事？
  - *Example*: **What the hell was that**? The flirting between Chandler and the delivery girl!
- **For shame!**: 真丢脸！太不应该了！
  - *Example*: And on your anniversary, **for shame**! How could you flirt with someone else?
- **No big deal**: 没什么大不了的。
  - *Example*: It's **no big deal**, I do it all the time. Just casual flirting.
- **Clear that up**: 澄清、弄清楚。
  - *Example*: I'm so glad we **cleared that up**. Now I understand the situation.
- **Go on**: 继续说、说吧。
  - *Example*: **Go on**, teach me something about men and women.

## 6. 剧情词汇
- **Ten-month anniversary**: 十个月纪念日
- **Ja George**: 一家高级餐厅名称
- **Law & Order**: 《法律与秩序》（知名美剧）
- **Order and Law**: Phoebe开玩笑说的剧名颠倒
- **Pepperoni**: 意大利辣香肠（披萨配料）
- **Vegetarian**: 素食主义者
- **Charm-O-Ross**: Ross自创的"魅力开关"（幽默说法）
- **Tri-State area**: 三州地区（纽约、新泽西、康涅狄格）
- **Sam Waterston**: 美国演员（Law & Order主演）
- **Crimes and Misdemeanors**: 《罪与错》（伍迪·艾伦电影）
- **Capricorn One**: 《摩羯星一号》（科幻电影）
- **Ground control to Major Tom**: David Bowie歌曲《Space Oddity》歌词
