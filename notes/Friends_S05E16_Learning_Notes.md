# Friends S05E16 口语表达笔记

## 1. 情绪与反应
- **Bolt upright**: 猛地坐起（通常因惊讶或惊吓）。
  - *Example*: He suddenly **bolted upright** in bed when he realized what happened.
- **Stunned**: 震惊的、目瞪口呆的。
  - *Example*: She was completely **stunned** by his unexpected confession.
- **Disgusted**: 厌恶的、恶心的。
  - *Example*: I'm **disgusted** at how messy this room is.

## 2. 决策与行动
- **Blow off**: 放弃、爽约（某事）。
  - *Example*: I guess I can **blow off** my acting class for you.
- **Take it myself**: 自己拿走/搬运。
  - *Example*: The delivery fee is too high, so I'll **take it myself**.
- **Call for backup**: 呼叫支援。
  - *Example*: If things go wrong, I'll **call for backup** immediately.
- **Run it through the computer**: 在电脑系统中查询。
  - *Example*: The police **ran the fingerprints through the computer** to find the owner.

## 3. 社交与关系
- **Go out**: 约会、交往。
  - *Example*: We **went out** for about six months back in college.
- **Crank it up a notch**: 提升关系等级、更进一步。
  - *Example*: Maybe we should **crank our friendship up a notch** and start dating.
- **Hit on sb.**: 搭讪、向某人示好。
  - *Example*: Don't **hit on your existing friends**! Meet someone new first.
- **Build a foundation**: 建立基础（指感情基础）。
  - *Example*: You need to **build a foundation** of friendship before dating.
- **Harbor a fugitive**: 窝藏逃犯。
  - *Example*: If you hide him, you could be charged with **harboring a fugitive**.

## 4. 描述事物
- **Freakishly strong**: 异常强壮的。
  - *Example*: Monica is **freakishly strong**, she can lift that couch easily.
- **Tight**: （关系）亲密的、紧密的。
  - *Example*: Chandler and Monica are really **tight** as a couple.
- **Irresistible**: 无法抗拒的、极具吸引力的。
  - *Example*: I suddenly became **irresistible** to her after saying that.
- **Reasonable man**: 通情达理的人。
  - *Example*: Look, I am a **reasonable man**, I'll accept store credit.

## 5. 实用短句
- **What's with him?**: 他怎么了？（询问某人异常行为）
  - *Example*: **What's with him**? He's been acting strange all day.
- **Are you kiddin'?**: 你在开玩笑吗？
  - *Example*: You want me to carry this? **Are you kiddin'?**
- **Something didn't quite add up**: 有些事情不太对劲/说不通。
  - *Example*: Their story **didn't quite add up**, so I suspected they were lying.
- **Worth the wait**: 值得等待。
  - *Example*: Once you find true love, it's **so worth the wait**.
- **For crying out loud**: 天哪、拜托（表示 frustration）。
  - *Example*: There were three of us **for crying out loud**!
- **I didn't see that coming**: 我没想到会这样。
  - *Example*: Wow! You're asking me out? **I didn't see that coming!**
- **Keep up the good work**: 继续保持、干得好。
  - *Example*: Thanks for helping. **Keep up the good work**!

## 6. 剧情词汇
- **Crossword puzzle**: 填字游戏
- **Maroon**: 褐红色、栗色
- **Connect the Dots**: 连点成画（儿童游戏）
- **Police badge**: 警徽
- **Ten Most Wanted lists**: 十大通缉犯名单
- **Vice**: 风化组（警察部门，负责处理卖淫等案件）
- **Undercover**: 卧底的
- **Precinct**: 警察局辖区
- **Homicide**: 凶杀案组
- **Pivot**: 旋转、 pivot（搬沙发时的经典台词）
- **Store credit**: 商店积分/抵用券
- **Slammer**: 监狱（俚语）
- **Book 'em**: 把他们抓起来/登记立案
- **Backup**: 后援、支援
- **Sipowicz**: 《NYPD Blue》电视剧中的角色名
- **Silver Spoons**: 《银匙》美国情景喜剧
