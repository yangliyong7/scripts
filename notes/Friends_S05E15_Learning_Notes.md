# Friends S05E15 口语表达笔记

## 1. 情绪与反应
- **Go on the lamb**: 逃跑、潜逃（俚语，指躲避追捕）。
  - *Example*: When Ross found out, Chandler tried to **go on the lamb** through the window.
- **Freak out**: 抓狂、惊慌失措（指对承诺感到恐惧）。
  - *Example*: Don't **freak out**! I'm telling you something you already know.
- **Baby fever**: 渴望生孩子的强烈愿望。
  - *Example*: You have **baby fever**! You're obsessed with marriage and babies.

## 2. 决策与行动
- **Make a big gesture**: 做出重大姿态（如求婚）。
  - *Example*: Unless you make some kind of **big gesture**, she won't forgive you.
- **Get down on one knee**: 单膝跪地（求婚姿势）。
  - *Example*: He slowly and awkwardly **got down on one knee** and proposed.
- **Chip in**: 凑钱、分担费用。
  - *Example*: Everyone in the building is **chipping in** a hundred bucks for the handyman.
- **Talk sb. up**: 在某人面前夸赞某人。
  - *Example*: I'm **talking you up to people** at the party. Just give it a little time.

## 3. 社交与关系
- **Lovebirds**: 情侣、恩爱夫妻（常带调侃语气）。
  - *Example*: Let these two **lovebirds** get back down to business.
- **Casual relationship**: 随意的恋爱关系（非正式交往）。
  - *Example*: I'm just hanging out with the girl that I'm seeing **casually**.
- **Relationship tutor**: 感情导师（指导对方如何处理关系）。
  - *Example*: I'm really tired of being your **relationship tutor**. Figure it out yourself.
- **Break up with sb.**: 与某人分手。
  - *Example*: I'm **breaking up with Katie** so I had to put on some extra padding.

## 4. 描述事物
- **Cheapskate**: 吝啬鬼、小气的人。
  - *Example*: President Steve told everyone that I was a **cheapskate**.
- **A man with principles**: 有原则的人。
  - *Example*: I'm just a guy who stands up for what he believes in. **A man with principles**.
- **Abrasive drunk**: 粗鲁的醉汉。
  - *Example*: When I first met Kurt, I thought he was an **abrasive drunk**.
- **Mind-numbingly stupid**: 愚蠢到让人麻木的。
  - *Example*: Lola is **mind-numbingly stupid**! I can't stand her.
- **Gold-digger**: 拜金女（为了钱而交往的人）。
  - *Example*: That girl is just a **gold-digger**. She only likes him for his money.
- **Cradle-robbing perv**: 老牛吃嫩草的变态（指责老年男性与年轻女性交往）。
  - *Example*: He's dating a 20-year-old? What a **cradle-robbing perv**!

## 5. 实用短句
- **What's up bro?**: 兄弟怎么了？（轻松的打招呼方式）。
  - *Example*: Hey Ross, **what's up bro**? Why are you running?
- **My bad**: 我的错、抱歉（非正式道歉）。
  - *Example*: Yeah, okay, **my bad**. I shouldn't have done that.
- **Get back down to business**: 继续做正事。
  - *Example*: Let's clear out and let these two **get back down to business**.
- **A few steps ahead of us**: 比我们提前了很多步（指讨论得太远）。
  - *Example*: Guys, you're **a few steps ahead of us**. We just started dating!
- **Don't judge sb. before you get to know them**: 在了解之前不要评判某人。
  - *Example*: **Don't judge Ross before you get to know him** all right?
- **I cannot look at it!**: 我看不下去了！（表示尴尬或震惊）。
  - *Example*: Ohhhh, **I cannot look at it**! This is so awkward!
- **That's not funny at all!**: 这一点都不好笑！
  - *Example*: **That's not funny at all!** Don't joke about marriage.

## 6. 剧情词汇
- **House warming gifts**: 乔迁礼物（Phoebe送给Ross的盐、面包和香薰蜡烛）
- **Tenants committee**: 租户委员会
- **Handyman**: 维修工（Howard，即将退休）
- **Nametag**: 名牌（Ross戴了两个名牌：Ross和Dr. Geller）
- **Godfather**: 教父（Joey想当Chandler和Monica孩子的教父）
- **Zero gravity moon steps**: 零重力月球步（Chandler形容大家想得太超前）
- **Breadstick fangs**: 面包棒假牙（Joey在餐厅逗Katie开心的把戏）
- **Extra padding**: 额外 padding（Joey穿了六件毛衣防打）
- **Dr. Geller**: Geller博士（Ross的博士头衔，他用来炫耀）
- **Party for Howard**: 为Howard举办的派对（与Ross的派对冲突）
- **Ring box**: 戒指盒（Chandler求婚时拿出的）
- **Chandler shaped hole**: Chandler形状的空洞（指他会逃跑时在门上留下的洞）
- **We were on a break**: 我们当时分手了（Ross和Rachel的经典梗）
