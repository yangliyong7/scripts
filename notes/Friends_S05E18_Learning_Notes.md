# Friends S05E18 口语表达笔记

## 1. 情绪与反应
- **Nerve wracking**: 令人紧张的、让人焦虑的。
  - *Example*: This audition is so **nerve-wracking**! I can't stop shaking.
- **Feel bad**: 感到难过、内疚。
  - *Example*: I **feel bad** that you didn't get the part.
- **Care about**: 关心、在乎。
  - *Example*: I **care more about** your success than my own career.

## 2. 决策与行动
- **Step aside**: 退让、退出。
  - *Example*: I think I'm just gonna **step aside** and let someone else take the lead.
- **Take credit for**: 把功劳归于自己。
  - *Example*: She always **takes credit for** other people's ideas.
- **Rue the day**: 后悔某一天（威胁用语）。
  - *Example*: Monica is gonna **rue the day** that she put me in charge of cups and ice.
- **Cut back**: 减少、削减。
  - *Example*: I am really, really trying to **cut back** on smoking.
- **Move on**: 继续前进、转移到下一个。
  - *Example*: We need to **move on** to the next candidate.

## 3. 社交与关系
- **Tag along**: 跟随、蹭饭者。
  - *Example*: You just **tagged along**! You're like the tag-a-long dad.
- **Go get 'em**: 去争取、加油打气。
  - *Example*: Good luck! **Go get 'em**! You'll do great!
- **Bum one**: 借一支烟。
  - *Example*: Excuse me, can I **bum one** of those cigarettes?
- **On board**: 赞同、支持。
  - *Example*: I am so **on board** with this idea! Let's do it!

## 4. 描述事物
- **Callback**: 试镜后被通知再次面试。
  - *Example*: I actually got a **callback** for the commercial!
- **Disgusting habit**: 恶心的习惯（指吸烟）。
  - *Example*: It's like I'm being punished for not having this **disgusting habit**.
- **Smell of success**: 成功的气息（讽刺用法）。
  - *Example*: This is actually the **smell of success**. (Rachel after smoking)
- **Intimate dinner party**: 亲密的晚宴派对。
  - *Example*: What happened to the **intimate dinner party** we planned?

## 5. 实用短句
- **What's up?**: 怎么了？有什么事？
  - *Example*: Hey Ross, **what's up**? You look upset.
- **That's it?**: 就这样？（表示不满或惊讶）
  - *Example*: **That's it?** You're just gonna let me do this?
- **Come on!**: 拜托！得了吧！
  - *Example*: **Come on!** Have you seen what my kid can do?!
- **Eh, what are you gonna do?**: 唉，你能怎么办呢？（无奈接受）
  - *Example*: He didn't get the part. **Eh, what are you gonna do?**
- **You lost me**: 我没听懂、你把我搞糊涂了。
  - *Example*: Wait, **you lost me**. Can you explain that again?

- **Commercial casting director**: 商业广告选角导演
- **Godfather**: 教父（Joey自称是Ben的教父）

- **Playstation**: 索尼游戏机

- **Tuscan style finger food**: 托斯卡纳风格手指食物
- **Patch Sisters**: 戒烟贴姐妹（Rachel给Kim和Nancy起的绰号）
- **Dry ice**: 干冰
- **Snow cones**: 刨冰
- **Sub-consciously**: 潜意识地
