# Friends S05E17 口语表达笔记

## 1. 情绪与反应
- **Miss read**: 误解、误会。
  - *Example*: I totally **misread** him and I didn't know what to do.
- **Freak out**: 抓狂、惊慌失措。
  - *Example*: You just **freaked out** about our relationship.
- **Feel the same way**: 有同样的感觉。
  - *Example*: I **feel the same way** about our relationship as you do.

## 2. 决策与行动
- **Get a shot**: 尝试、给一次机会。
  - *Example*: I may regret this but I'm going to **give you a shot** for the job.
- **Nail the interview**: 成功完成面试。
  - *Example*: If you **nail the interview**, you'll definitely get the job.
- **Take care of sth.**: 负责处理某事。
  - *Example*: I've **taken care of everything** for the party already.

## 3. 社交与关系
- **Flirt with sb.**: 与某人调情。
  - *Example*: This totally hot girl in Ross's building is **flirting with me**.
- **All over each other**: 对彼此非常亲昵。
  - *Example*: We're **all over each other** all the time.
- **Beat them**: 打败他们（竞争心理）。
  - *Example*: We have **got to beat them**! We're the hotter couple!

## 4. 描述事物
- **Hot girl/boy**: 火辣的/帅气的男孩/女孩。
  - *Example*: I'm looking for that **hot girl** who waved at me.
- **Worth it**: 值得。
  - *Example*: It's the perfect, perfect job for me, it's totally **worth it**.
- **Parallel universe**: 平行宇宙（比喻难以找到的地方）。
  - *Example*: She lives in some kind of **hot girl parallel universe**, it's impossible to find her apartment.

## 5. 实用短句
- **What are you talking about?**: 你在说什么？
  - *Example*: **What are you talking about**? I don't understand what you mean.
- **That's not the point**: 这不是重点。
  - *Example*: Sure, it's a nice gift, but **that's not the point**.
- **Get all your facts**: 弄清楚所有事实。
  - *Example*: Why don't you just **get all your facts** before you run around telling everybody?
