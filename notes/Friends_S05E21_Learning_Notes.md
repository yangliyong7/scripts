# Friends S05E21 口语表达笔记

## 1. 情绪与反应
- **Pull off**: 成功做到、驾驭（某种风格或行为）。
  - *Example*: I don't think I can **pull off** a beard; it looks weird on me.
- **Freak out**: （因恐惧或紧张）惊慌失措、抓狂。
  - *Example*: She **freaks out** whenever anything comes close to her eyes.
- **Flip out**: 大发雷霆、情绪失控。
  - *Example*: The cat just **flipped out** and scratched me when I tried to pet it.
- **Get riled up**: 变得激动、生气或烦躁。
  - *Example*: The dogs **got riled up** when they saw the stranger at the door.

## 2. 决策与行动
- **Move forward**: 推进、向前发展（常指关系）。
  - *Example*: I really want to **move this relationship forward** by moving in together.
- **Jump into something**: 仓促开始某事、贸然行动。
  - *Example*: Let's not **jump into** a big decision without thinking it through.
- **Split the cost**: 分摊费用。
  - *Example*: If we live together, we can **split the cost** of rent and utilities.
- **Haggle**: 讨价还价。
  - *Example*: You obviously know how to **haggle**, so I'll lower the price to $800.
- **Rob sb. blind**: 敲竹杠、宰人（让人花冤枉钱）。
  - *Example*: They're **robbing you blind**! That coffee costs way too much.

## 3. 社交与关系
- **Hold it in**: 憋住、忍住（不说或不做某事）。
  - *Example*: He made me promise not to tell, but I couldn't **hold it in** any longer!
- **Make sb. a man**: 让某人更有男子气概/成熟。
  - *Example*: Talk to him and make him scared like you—**make him a man**!
- **Be on the same team**: 站在同一战线、是一伙的。
  - *Example*: Monica! Stop throwing it so hard! We're **on the same team**!
- **Call the cops**: 报警、叫警察。
  - *Example*: Give me my money back, or I'll **call the cops** because you're robbing me!

## 4. 描述事物
- **Purebred, show-quality**: 纯种的、展览级别的（形容宠物）。
  - *Example*: This is a **purebred, show-quality** Sphinx cat, that's why it's expensive.
- **Extravagant**: 奢侈的、铺张浪费的。
  - *Example*: Buying a $1,000 cat was a little **extravagant**, but I got a good deal.
- **Hyper-competitive**: 过度好胜的、竞争意识超强的。
  - *Example*: Monica became **hyper-competitive** during the ball game and wouldn't let anyone rest.
- **Compromised**: 受损的、受影响的（此处指视力受影响）。
  - *Example*: My vision has been **compromised** because the cat made my eyes water!

## 5. 实用短句
- **Check it out**: 瞧瞧这个、看一下。
  - *Example*: **Check it out**! I bought a new cat. (Or: Look at this cool gadget!)
- **Way to go**: 干得好、做得棒。
  - *Example*: You got the lead in a movie? **Way to go**, Joey!
- **I give up**: 我放弃了、我认输。
  - *Example*: **I give up**! I don't know what to do with this aggressive cat.
- **Suck it up**: 忍着点、硬撑下去（口语）。
  - *Example*: Come on guys! **Suck it up**! We're closing in on ten hours!
- **Look alive**: 打起精神来、活跃点。
  - *Example*: **Look alive**, Monica! Don't drop the ball now!
- **As long as we're here**: 既然我们都在这儿了（顺便做某事）。
  - *Example*: Do you want to eat out? No, let's eat here **as long as we're here**.

- **Sphinx cat**: 斯芬克斯猫（无毛猫）
- **Litter box**: 猫砂盆
- **Mesozoic Mastodon**: 中生代乳齿象（Ross 说的古生物）
- **Paleozoic Amphibian**: 古生代两栖动物
- **Brooklyn Heights**: 布鲁克林高地（纽约的一个高档社区）
- **The Post**: 《纽约邮报》（New York Post）
- **Oven mitt**: 烤箱手套、隔热手套
- **Store credit**: 商店积分/抵用券（退货时不给现金，只给店内消费额度）
- **Red-velvet pillow**: 红色天鹅绒枕头（Gunther 为猫买的）
