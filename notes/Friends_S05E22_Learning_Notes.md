# Friends S05E22 口语表达笔记

## 1. 情绪与反应
- **Freak out**: 惊慌失措、抓狂。
  - *Example*: Don't **freak out**, but I think I lost your keys.
- **Push my buttons**: 惹我生气、踩雷区。
  - *Example*: He knows exactly how to **push my buttons**.
- **Be furious with sb.**: 对某人极度愤怒。
  - *Example*: She was **furious with** me for forgetting her birthday.
- **Sick**: (口语) 你有病吧/真变态。
  - *Example*: You like pineapple on pizza? You are **sick**!
- **Get hopes up**: 抱太大希望。
  - *Example*: I don't want to **get my hopes up** about the promotion.

## 2. 决策与行动
- **Back to square one**: 回到起点、从头再来。
  - *Example*: The code didn't compile, so we are **back to square one**.
- **Place a bet**: 下注、打赌。
  - *Example*: I'm going to **place a bet** on the red team.
- **Spring for**: 破费、舍得花钱买（奢侈品）。
  - *Example*: It's your birthday, so I'll **spring for** dinner tonight.
- **Dump all over**: 泼冷水、全盘否定。
  - *Example*: Don't **dump all over** my idea before I finish explaining it.
- **Hang out**: 待着、闲逛、等待。
  - *Example*: Do you want to **hang out** at my place this weekend?

## 3. 社交与关系
- **Caught in the middle**: 夹在中间（为难）。
  - *Example*: I hate being **caught in the middle** of their arguments.
- **Got off the phone**: 刚挂电话。
  - *Example*: I just **got off the phone** with the client; they are happy.
- **Make a fortune**: 发大财、赚大钱。
  - *Example*: If this app takes off, we could **make a fortune**.

## 4. 描述事物
- **Chilling**: 令人毛骨悚然的。
  - *Example*: That horror movie was absolutely **chilling**.
- **Independent film**: 独立电影（小成本）。
  - *Example*: We watched an interesting **independent film** last night.
- **Freakishly strong**: 力气大得离谱。
  - *Example*: He is **freakishly strong**; he lifted that box with one hand.

## 5. 实用短句
- **Let's get this over with**: 速战速决吧（做讨厌的事时）。
  - *Example*: I hate dentists, but **let's get this over with**.
- **Is this okay with you?**: 你没问题吧？/ 这样可以吗？
  - *Example*: I'll pick you up at 8 PM. **Is this okay with you?**
- **I'm out of here**: 我走了 / 我撤了。
  - *Example*: It's getting late. **I'm out of here**.
- **Worth a shot**: 值得一试。
  - *Example*: I don't know if it will work, but it's **worth a shot**.
